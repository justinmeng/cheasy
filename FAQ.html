Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is the website called "Cheasy"?

Our group came up with that punny name from the combination of "cheap" and "easy". It was so bad, it was good.



Q: What is the premise of this website?

The goal of this website is to allow people to search for common recipes and easily find the costs
of each item easily without having to search each item one at a time on their preferred search engine.



Q: Will you add more recipes in the future?

If the site gets popular enough, we may consider adding more recipes that are requested by the users of the site.



Q: Are there any plans of implementing other supermarket APIs?

There's always a possibility! We want our users to have access to the best (and most preferably cheapest)
ingredients out there, and a way to do so is to have a wide selection of markets at hand.

What do we do?

We're dedicated to finding cheap food at your local Target.

Click on the "RECIPES" button on top to get access to

delicious meals at low costs!
